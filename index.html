Rajiv Shekhar, Director

Rajiv Shekhar (born 1960)[4] is a professor and director at IIT(ISM) Dhanbad. Prior to joining IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, he was working as a professor at the Department of Materials Science and Engineering, IIT Kanpur.[2] In recent times, he has been actively working towards research related to Solar thermal energy and applications.[5][6]
